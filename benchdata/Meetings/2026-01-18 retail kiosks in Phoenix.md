# Retail Kiosks in Phoenix

## Overview
Discussed the initial deployment plan for the new retail kiosks across three Phoenix mall locations. The team wants a steady launch rather than a same-week rollout so installers can handle site issues without slipping the broader timeline. Current scope covers customer-facing touch screens, payment hardware, and basic enclosure branding.

## Operations
Shipment timing remains the main risk. Two kiosk frames are already packed, but the remaining screens are still waiting on final warehouse checks. Installers asked for clearer receiving contacts at each site so they can avoid delays when freight arrives. ## Launch Timeline
Target launch is late February if shipment leaves on time next week. The proposed timeline starts with one pilot location, followed by the other two sites after a three-day review window. If the pilot exposes issues with screen calibration or payment setup, the team will pause the second wave until fixes are confirmed.
