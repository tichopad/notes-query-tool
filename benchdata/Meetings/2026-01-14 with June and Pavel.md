# 2026-01-14 Deployment Planning – Eldorado Digital Signage

Met with June and Pavel to lock down the rollout plan before hardware ships.

## Rollout Plan

Installers will get mounting diagrams on Monday. Hardware leaves the warehouse Monday afternoon, should reach Eldorado by Wednesday. Every display ships with a pre-labeled player, power kit, and bracket pack. Player config will be standardized before shipment: kiosk mode on, remote management active, local fallback playlist loaded, auto-restart at 4 a.m.

## Millenium Garden

Millenium Garden is going first because it has the cleanest cabling path and the simplest wall conditions. Target is six screens total — lobby displays, entry wayfinding panels, and two shared amenity screens. Final mounting Thursday, content verification Friday. If the freight truck gets delayed by weather, installers start with demo units on-site and swap to production units after delivery.

## Deadline

Full launch by January 23. Checklist before go-live:
- confirm shipment receipt
- verify installer access window
- validate mounts
- pair each screen with the correct player
- test network registration
- confirm orientation
- publish launch content
- photo proof for the deployment log

Pavel will be point of contact on-site. June is handling content sign-off.
